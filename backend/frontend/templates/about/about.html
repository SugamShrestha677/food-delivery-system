Our Story

Born from a simple idea: great food should be accessible to everyone, everywhere.

In 2020, our founders Sarah and Marcus noticed a gap in the food delivery market. While technology was advancing rapidly, the human element was being lost. Restaurants struggled with high commission fees, delivery drivers faced poor working conditions, and customers dealt with unreliable service.

We set out to build something different—a platform that puts community first. By offering fair partnerships with restaurants, competitive earnings for drivers, and transparent pricing for customers, we're creating a sustainable ecosystem that benefits everyone.

Today, we're proud to serve over 50,000 customers across 25+ cities, working with more than 1,200 restaurant partners and supporting thousands of delivery drivers in building their own businesses.
